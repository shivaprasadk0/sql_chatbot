# Example Questions and Answers for AI Knowledge Assistant

Q: How many users are registered in the system?
A: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM users;

Q: List all orders made by user with id 5.
A: SELECT * FROM orders WHERE user_id = 5;

Q: Show the name and email of all users.
A: SELECT name, email FROM users;

Q: How many orders have been placed today?
A: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM orders WHERE DATE(order_date) = CURDATE();

Q: Get all products ordered with quantity more than 10.
A: SELECT * FROM orders WHERE quantity > 10;
